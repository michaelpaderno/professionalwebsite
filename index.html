Michael Paderno

MBA candidate at the Wharton School, Class of 2028. I've built my career in strategy, across consulting, a startup of my own, and advertising technology, and I write about the business of entertainment.

Most recently, I worked in strategy and special projects at Mediaocean, an advertising technology platform processing more than $300B in annual ad spend. My work there spanned AI initiatives across the organization, strategic partnerships, and go-to-market with partners including Disney and Comcast. Before that, I co-founded Creator Space, a startup helping content creators monetize directly with their audiences, and began my career at McKinsey & Company.

In 2025 and 2026, I supported the Sundance Film Festival as an independent pro bono strategy consultant, analyzing the economics of the festival's online offering. Outside of work, I've performed standup at the Comedy Cellar, and my short-form video work has passed a million views.
